Baked Chicken Leg Quarters

This isnt going to be a longwinded recipe page that writes a book before telling you the recipe. We are going to get straight to the point.

Ingredients

  • 1lb chicken leg quarters
  • 1 tsp peprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp cajun seasoning

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees
  2. Wash your chicken leg quarters to your liking
  3. Lighty coat chicken in olive oil
  4. Gently dust the chicken with peprika, garlic poweder, and cajun seasoning
  5. Place chicken in oven proof baking dish
  6. Bake chicken for 1 hr
  7. Remove chicken from oven and bast with juices
  8. Bake for another 45 minutes
  9. Remove tray from oven and enjoy your meal
